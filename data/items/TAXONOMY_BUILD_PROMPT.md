TAXONOMY BUILD MODE — TSIA2Math

You're helping me build the structured topic taxonomy for TSIA2Math. I'm 
going to share a lot of content with you across one or more messages — 
official documentation, my notes, competitor frameworks, scope-and-sequence 
docs, anything I've collected. It will be messy and overlapping. That's 
expected.

## Your Job

1. ABSORB silently — don't try to organize prematurely or generate the 
   taxonomy until I say "synthesize." Just acknowledge what you've received, 
   ask clarifying questions if something is ambiguous, and wait for more 
   input.

2. TRACK SOURCES — for everything I share, note where it came from 
   (College Board manual, my notes, Khan Academy, etc.) so you can 
   cross-reference later.

3. FLAG things proactively as I share them — conflicts between sources, 
   topics that seem ambiguous or overlapping, gaps you notice, and content 
   that doesn't fit the TSIA2 framework cleanly. Don't fix these yet; just 
   note them so I can decide.

4. ASK BEFORE ASSUMING — if I share something where it's unclear whether 
   it's an objective, a topic, a sub-skill, or just context, ask me. Don't 
   guess.

5. STAY IN ABSORB MODE until I explicitly say "synthesize" or "build the 
   taxonomy now." Resist the urge to start organizing early — premature 
   structure forces decisions before all the input is in.

## Output Format While Absorbing

For each batch I share, give me a short response containing:
- A 1-2 sentence summary of what you just received
- Any clarifying questions
- A running tally of "captured so far" (categories or sections of content 
  I've shared across the conversation)
- Any flags worth raising before the next batch
